1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Cotopia ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal and usage data in connection with the Forth Connect AI Product, website, demo, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). It applies to users, customers, and visitors ("you", "your").

2. Scope

This policy covers data collected:

  • During purchase, download, and licensing of the Product;
  • When you use the Product locally, hosted demos, or Cotopia-hosted services;
  • On Cotopia websites, documentation portals, and support channels;
  • Via telemetry or diagnostics if you opt in (see Section 6).

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Data You Provide

  • Account & purchase: name, company, billing information, email, payment transaction details (handled by payment processors).
  • Support & communications: messages, attachments, and troubleshooting logs you submit to support.
  • Content you upload: customization files, contract ABIs, or code you share with Cotopia for professional services.

3.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage & analytics: IP address, browser/OS, device identifiers, pages visited, demo interactions, feature usage, timestamps.
  • Crash reports & diagnostics: stack traces, error messages, performance metrics (may include partial environment details).
  • On‑chain publicly available blockchain addresses, transaction hashes, and on‑chain events for services where Cotopia indexes events (only public data).

3.3 Third‑Party Data

We may receive data from third parties such as payment processors, analytics providers, and identity verification services consistent with the providers' privacy practices.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Services, deliver downloads and license keys.
  • To process payments, verify purchases, and prevent fraud (via payment processors).
  • To provide support, respond to inquiries, and investigate issues.
  • To send administrative messages, product updates, security notices, and marketing where you consent.
  • To analyze usage patterns, optimize product UX, and prioritize feature development.
  • To comply with legal obligations, enforce our agreements, and defend Cotopia’s rights.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (EU/GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, our lawful bases include:

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to provide the Services and fulfill purchase agreements.
  • Legitimate interests — product improvement, fraud prevention, analytics, and security (we balance these interests against your rights).
  • Consent — for optional telemetry/marketing where we request consent.
  • Legal compliance — where required by law.

6. Telemetry, Analytics & Opt‑In

Cotopia may offer optional telemetry to collect crash reports, performance metrics, and anonymous usage statistics. Telemetry is disabled by default in shipped templates unless you enable it. When enabled, we will describe what data is collected and provide controls to opt out or remove collected telemetry data.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our websites may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics, session management, and preferences. Third‑party services (e.g., Google Analytics, Plausible) may set cookies — consult those providers' privacy policies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service providers: payment processors, hosting providers, analytics vendors, and support platforms acting on our behalf.
  • Legal & safety: to comply with legal processes, prevent fraud, or protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers: in connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales (with notice to affected users as required).
  • Public on‑chain Cotopia may index or publish public blockchain events related to demo contracts; public blockchain data is not considered private.

We require vendors to protect data consistent with this policy and applicable law.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and for legitimate business purposes. Retention periods vary by data type (purchase records retained for accounting and tax compliance; support tickets retained until resolved plus an archival period).

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • Access: request copies of personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: request deletion of personal data (subject to legal or contractual retention obligations).
  • Portability: request a machine‑readable copy of data you provided.
  • Objection/Restriction: object to processing or request restrictions where grounds exist.
  • Opt‑out of marketing and revoke consent for optional processing.

To exercise rights, contact support@cotopia.org. We may require verification and will respond within applicable legal timeframes.

11. International Transfers

Data may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. When transferring personal data from the EEA/UK, Cotopia will rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) or other lawful transfer mechanisms where required.

12. Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. No security is perfect — we cannot guarantee absolute security. Report suspected incidents to support@cotopia.org.

13. Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or local minimum age). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child without valid parental consent, we will take steps to delete it.

14. Privacy for On‑chain Interactions

Blockchain transactions are public. Any wallet addresses, transaction hashes, or contract interactions you perform on-chain are visible on public block explorers and may be associated with your identity by third parties. Cotopia cannot delete or control public blockchain data. If you use Cotopia-hosted indexers or dashboards, we will document what on-chain data is stored and how it is displayed.

15. Third‑Party Links & Embedded Content

We aim to comply with applicable privacy laws. For California residents: information about categories of personal data collected, sources, purposes, and sharing is available on request. To submit a CCPA request contact support@cotopia.org and include your name, email, and verify identity.
